Gun Detection Model

Welcome to our Gun Detection Model! Our group consisting of Aditya Shubham, Gaurav Srivastava, and Vasudev Sharma, have developed a deep learning model that detects guns in images. The model can be used for various applications, including law enforcement and public safety.

Use Cases

  • Law enforcement and public safety
  • Security systems in public places such as airports, malls, and schools
  • Preventing illegal possession of firearms
